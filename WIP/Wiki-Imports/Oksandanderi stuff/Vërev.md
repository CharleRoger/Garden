The city of **Vërev** is a major city within the [[Yarlish Lowlands]]. Several important dynasties originated from here, such as the [[Second Dynasty of Vërev]], who founded the [[Dominion]] of Vërev and later the [[Astmergian Empire]]. A second major dynasty from Vërev would ultimately also establish the [[Neo-Astmergian Empire]].

## History

### Ancient History

It is unknown when the city of Vërev was founded. Most estimates range between 1200 BGS till 700 BGS. Legend says, that Vërev was founded by the legendary hero [[Verbayn]]. The city's temple contains the alleged tomb of Verbayn.

### Pre-Dominion

Before becoming a major power Vërev was ruled, like many cities at the time, by a council of [[Highpriests]]. Around the [[3rd century BGS]] this rule was called into question. In parallel, in many other cities the soldier caste was gaining power and influence. In [[160 BGS]] Vërev became a duarchy. The priests and the soldier caste of the city would each elect one leader every 15 years. This arrangement however deteriorated quickly. In [[82 BGS]] the highpriest was overthrown by [[Sasgort]] of Vërev, who would become the founder of the [[First Dynasty of Vërev]]. The first dynasty lasted for somewhat over a century, but overall did not amount to much. Its last [[king]] [[Atmur]] of Vërev was overthrown in [[52 AGS]] by Kalyardun, who would go on to establish the [[Second Dynasty of Vërev]].

### Dominion of Vërev

Under Kalyardun's reign, Vërev would see much more success and a rapid ascension to power. One of the most important contributors was an alliance with the city of [[Tarin]]. Kalyardun started his reign by expansion and kickstarted a series of wars of conquest, which would last for almost a century, called the [[Vërevian Wars]]. Kalyardun was succeeded by his son [[Sarovagas]], who would continue these conquests and finally by [[Astmerge the Great]] himself. When Astmerge became king of Vërev, Vërev was among the most powerful cities in the Lowlands and only rivaled by [[Kamag]] and the [[Kingdom of Erme and Vukhun]].

### Astmergian Dominion

With the declaration of the [[Astmergian Empire]], Vërev would take a slight backseat, but was still regarded as the second most important city in the realm. However many nobles in Vërev remained contentious and disliked many of Astmerge's decisions. Many of the Vërevian elite treated the Astmergian Empire as the Vërevian Empire instead. When Astmerge passed away they chose to declare his oldest living son [[Yardunmud]] as new Emperor. This caused an immediate split between them and [[Kalotgane]], where [[Kesërn]] and his wife [[Khayanmari]], daughter of Astmerge, were declared Emperor and Empress. The conflict between Vërev and Kalotgane ended finally with the destruction of Kalotgane in 267. The last emperor, [[Yardunadi]] was taken hostage and held in Vërev until his death in [[314]]. Although Vërev was victorious in this conflict, its success would not last long. Soon other cities chipped away at its territories, until only the city's core territory was left. The 2nd Dynasty remained in power until centuries later and would only go extinct in the [[7th century]]
