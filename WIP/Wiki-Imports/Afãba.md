### Vowel harmony

Afãba exhibits a progressive assimilation rule in which all vowels in a word must match the closeness of the vowel in the initial syllable, resulting in a system of [vowel harmony](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vowel_harmony "w:Vowel harmony").
