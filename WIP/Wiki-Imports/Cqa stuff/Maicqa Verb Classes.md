### Verbs of Attendance (ATT, Ⅰ)

Attending stuff generally; nonstandard events.

- Meeting up somewhere, keeping an agreement.
- Experiencing something exceptional (e.g. coma)
- Voluntary movement (doing by choice).

_Intransitive/Monotransitive_

### Verbs of Experience (EXP, Ⅱ)

Observing, experiencing…

- Observing something you’re not part of.
- Observing completely generic events.

- Hear, see, smell, hear word of...

_Intransitive/Monotransitive/Ditransitive(share experience; show)_

### Verbs of Maintenance (MNT, Ⅲ)

Maintaining, taking care of; small, recurrent, often “positive” events.

- Tending to, ensuring the integrity of something; checking up on.
- Gradually growing something (e.g. building up painting skill)
- Regular maintenance tasks; maintaining sleep, eating, drinking.
- Repairing something, putting back together.
- Supporting, nourishing; agreeing with.

_Monotransitive (sometimes reflexive)/Ditransitive(doing for reason)_

### Verbs of Adjustment (AJST, Ⅳ)

Tweaking, “small” interactions

- Non-iterative maintenance, slight improvement/change.
- Turn a knob, tie a knot, brush your hair, mark a bag (write for brief utility).

_Monotransitive/Ditransitive(doing for reason)_

### Verbs of Interaction (NTRA, Ⅴ)

Social interactions

- Talk to someone, hug them, sing a song.
- Expressing feelings, arguing.
- Give?

_Intransitive/Monotransitive/Ditransitive_

### Verbs of Assembly (ASSM, Ⅵ)

Assemble from pieces, put together, piece together; artificial growth “assembly”

- Construct, bind together items, saddle up a mollusk.
- Glue together sth new.
- Put together a report, academic paper, whatever; write for detail.
- Expressing rationale, debating, haggling, negotiating.
- Learn/teach, “(make) put together knowledge”.

_Monotransitive/Ditransitive_

### Verbs of Infliction (IFL, Ⅶ)

Inflicting, physically interacting, “big” interactions

- “Impose upon” something an idea, an action...

- Altering objects in larger ways; Bending a piece of snot, moving a bag.
- Single actions that affect the world around you; one instance of tax fraud.
- Weather also does this.

_Monotransitive/Ditransitive_
