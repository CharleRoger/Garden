The **Agacam languages** are a group of tonal [[Koyanic languages]] spoken in the plains of [[Riverline]]. They are related to the [[Hma'ma languages]], together forming a primary Koyanic branch known as [[Katic]].

## Name

_Agacam_ is a common endonym used among speakers of the languages, with corresponding singular form _kagacam_. The lexical stem _-gacam_ may be related to the name [[Qachkav]], via the suggested Proto-Koyanic reconstruction *qx₂ə́c-kʰəmə "one-people".
