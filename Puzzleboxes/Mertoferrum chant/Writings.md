By Sertaxa, a secular philosopher from early second century AGS:
> And there are those who left the temples, meeting in normal houses. It is said they worship not "Fecul", but "One". They respond they worship the one divinity [xalca] common to all gods and thus honor all gods. Much suspicion has been directed towards them, especially from the totyq, for they meet only among their own kind and sing secret [[nona|songs]] of praise no one else may hear. Yet they are invariably from among the Wise, and in good standing with the King.

By King Raxa, who was installed after Feculky lost the first [[Totky-Feculky wars|Totky-Feculky war]]:
>Given in the tenth year of King Raxa of [[Feculky]] and approved by the totyq. Read this in the town square and behind the doors of the nonatashouses: all nonatasyq must leave [same word as in "leave the city"] singing and praise Fecul in the temple or leave the city. After 12 days, any nonatas singing in the city of Fecul will be xal in the name of Fecul. For they (singular) have given Fecul nothing, and have brought great wrath upon Feculky, they will be given to Fecul. Should anyone stay or help a nonatas stay, they (singular) will be struck by all the forces of the King.
