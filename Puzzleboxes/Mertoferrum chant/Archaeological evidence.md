## Grave I
Found in northern [[Taspi]].

**Wooden spear**
Carbon-dated to late first century AGS with 90% confidence. No traces of blood on the flint spearhead.

**16 Small stones**
All carved to be circular in shape.

**Bronze plate**
The following is written counterclockwise:
> The wise pray: O Pilur, who has been xal, and is xal now and forever - help me see the truth and guard us from blindless. We take these fruit and divide (*xaloty*) them: profane fruit for the profane people, xal fruit for the xal gods. Like for like: profane for profane and xal for xal. And as we have given, we trust you give. O Pilur, who helps us see the truth and guards us from blindless - we praise you now and forever! 

**Vase**
Has a removable cap. Residue of ashes.

## Grave II
Found in Serquky. 

**Broken wooden spear**
Carbon-dated to first century BGS with 90% confidence. Traces of blood on the flint spearhead.

**Shield**
Depicts a crab along with thunderbolts. Inscription: "In this life and next, I always fight for you."

**Skeleton**
Relatively well preserved. Some bones are broken.

## Grave III
Found in Kypky.

**Dagger**
Unable to be dated due to being entirely made out of metal. No traces of blood. Inscription: Xal fruit for the xal gods.

**Golden plate**
Residue of fruits found on top of the plate. Scratches, possibly from the dagger.

**Vase**
Entirely sealed. Hollow inside. Not opened. Carvings of crabs on the outside. One crab is drawn higher than the rest and in more detail.
