
## Grave I
Found in northern [[Taspi]].

**Wooden spear**
Carbon-dated to late first century AGS with 90% confidence. No traces of blood on the flint spearhead.

**16 Small stones**
All carved to be circular in shape.

**Bronze plate**
The following is written counterclockwise:
> The wise pray: Pilur, who has been xal, and is xal now and forever - help me see the truth and guard us from blindless. We take these fruit and divide (*xaloty*) them: profane fruit for the profane people, xal fruit for the xal gods. Like for like: profane for profane and xal for xal. And as we have given, we trust you give. O Pilur, who helps us see the truth and guards us from blindless - we praise you now and forever! 

**Vase**
Has a removable cap. Residue of ashes.
