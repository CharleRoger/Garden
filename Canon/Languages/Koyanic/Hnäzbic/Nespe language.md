#language/koyanic #hnäzb #nespe #uhustaw

**Nespe** is a [[Hnäzb languages|Hnäzbic]] language, spoken by the inhabitants of the [[Uhustaw]] (Nespe: *[[Uhustaw#Name|Čüüwän]]*). 
Nespe is slightly more divergent than the usual [[Hnäzb languages]], due to its early isolation and different lifestyle of its speakers.

# Development from Proto-Hnäzb

Cluster devoicing:

- Obstruent clusters are devoiced. 

Lateral reduction:

- Lateral affricates > Postalveolar affricates 

Unconditioned mergers (No clusters):

- qʰ > χ
- q' > q
- q > ʔ
- pʰ > h 
- z > r

Older stages of Nespe retained length distinctions from the [[Proto-Hnäzb language]], but later it merged with stress. Now length distinctions have become synonymous with accent and stress.
