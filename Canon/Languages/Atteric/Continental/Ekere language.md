#language/atteric #ekere #kóty 

**Ekere** is an [[Atteric languages|Atteric language]] which was spoken in and around the [[Bay of Lokkat]].

# Name
Ekere was natively called *Ekere haraga* [e̞ke̞ɾe̞ ha̠ɾa̠ga̠].
