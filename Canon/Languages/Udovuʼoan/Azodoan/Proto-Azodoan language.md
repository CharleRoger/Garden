#language/udovuoan #udovuo #xiyeru 

The **Proto-Azodoan language** is the hypothetical reconstructed ancestor of [[Ahōtoli]] and related language varieties of [[Azodo]], one of the five home-islands of [[Udovuʼo]].

| ^     | Short       | -   | **\*i** | **\*ə**    | **\*u** |
| ^     | Extra-short | -   | **\*ĭ** | **\*ə̆**   | **\*ŭ** |
| Open  | Long        | -   | **\*ē** | (**\*ā**?) | **\*ō** |
| ^     | Short       | -   | **\*e** | **\*a**    | **\*o** |

Proto-Azodoan seems to have already developed the three vowel lengths which characterise [[Ahōtoli|Ahōtoli's]] unusual vowel system, though the precise qualities are not known and it is possible that the long vowels were in fact still diphthongs.
