**Ásademóku** ([aːsade̞ˈmo̞ːkʉ]) is the main language spoken on Ásadedzó. It is related to [[Kumomóku]] with which it is mutually intelligible.

## Etymology

The name _Ásademóku_ is likely shortened from _Ásadedzómóku_ "Ásadedzó language".

## Phonology

The dialect described here is spoken around 0 AGS.

### Consonants

Ásademóku has 31 consonant phonemes shown in the table below along with two special phonemes /Q/ and /N/ whereas /Q/ induces gemination in the following consonant and /N/ is a nasal consonant that assimilates to the place of articulation of the consonant immediately following it including across word boundaries assuming there is no prosodic break. For example, /Np Nt Nk/ are realised as [mp nt ŋk]. If there is no such consonant it is realised as [ŋ].
