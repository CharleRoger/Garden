**Fanaʼažimyu** was the semi-legendary first [[Āeruweʼa]] in the [[Xiyeru]] historical tradition. She was a member of the last generation of [[Tarodaroeru]], the ancient people who are said to have founded the [[Xiyeru]] culture.

# Name

*[[Xiri dictionary#fanaʼažimyu|Fa-naʼa-žimyu]]* means "who speaks the truth", a [[Ńuʼu#Mūʼojūńuʼu|mūʼojūńuʼu]] granted in honour of her recitation of ancestral lore, said to have been handed down from the original [[Ībarovā|celestial creator deities]] through generations of [[Sārovā|lesser spirits]].
