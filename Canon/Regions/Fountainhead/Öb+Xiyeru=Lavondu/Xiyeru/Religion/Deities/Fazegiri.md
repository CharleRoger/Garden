**Fazegiri** is one of the [[ībarovā]], celestial creator deities in [[Xiyeru religion]], associated with the planet [[Tünd]].

# Mythology

Fazegiri was one of the second three [[ībarovā]] to emerge from [[Fururūʼupwa]] in the [[Xiyeru creation story#The early universe|early universe]]. They are said to have granted humanity the ability to speak.

They are the patron deity of language, storytelling, darkness, and justice.

Fazegiri bore three children with [[Faraʼeība]]: **[[Āwirō]]**, **[[Āhiñu]]** and **[[Āraʼe]]**.
# Name

The name *[[Xiri dictionary#fazegiri|fa-zegi-ri]]* means "who gives the gift of language".

In [[Möxali]], they are called *[[Möxali dictionary#zigil|Zigil]]*, one of the six [[Zívon|lunar months]] of the [[Öcídñox]] calendar.
