The **Āviwinēs** or **Āviwinēs ībažeʼe** is the zodiac of 16 constellations along the ecliptic in [[Xiyeru astronomy]], through which the [[sun]], [[Solar system#Planets|visible planets]] and [[Winter#Moons|moons of Winter]] were seen to travel in a cyclic course.

# Etymology

*[[Xiri dictionary#āviwinēs|Ā-viwi-nēs]] [[Xiri dictionary#ība|ība-žeʼe]]* means "Great-ring-road of the sky".
